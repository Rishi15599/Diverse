ABOUT

Established in 1992, Diverse Inspection Services, Inc. is a family founded and run business. Our main focus is providing as many aspects of home inspection services while maintaining an utmost level of thoroughness, honesty, and integrity to you, our valued client. Whether you are a first time home buyer, home seller, realtor, or homeowner you will be given an unbiased and unparalleled level of attention to the property in question. No two homes are the same therefore they should not be looked at the same.

We service San Francisco, the Peninsula, and East Bay areas. Appointments are scheduled Monday through Friday but for special circumstances can be scheduled for weekends as well. Rates do vary depending on property type, size, location, and services requested. Please feel free to contact us for additional details and information.

Licensing affiliations and/or memberships: Structural Pest Control Board of California (SPCB); Pest Control Operators of California (PCOC); Contractors State License Board (CSLB); American Society of Home Inspectors (ASHI); San Francisco Residential Energy & Water Conservation Inspections (SF CREI)

OUR TEAM

Diverse Inspection Services, Inc. is currently run by two brothers, Marco and Devan Donaldson. As native San Franciscans, they grew up in the industry while witnessing and learning from the broad changes of the City and Bay Area. From an early age they have had a multitude of exposure and experiences to general construction, dry rot repairs, real estate, and the many nuances of inspecting a home. This gives them a unique and special understanding of San Francisco and Bay Area properties.
